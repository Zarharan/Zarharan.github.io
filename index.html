About

I am a M.Sc Artificial Intelligence student at Iran University of Science and Technology. I am currently working for an Artificial Intelligence company as a director of AI team, which is called Dadmatech. We are trying to implement a Persian chit chat model. Besides, I had submitted a proposal about detecting Persian fake news for Iran's National Elites Foundation. Consequently, I have been managing this project since November 2019 and as a result, we have developed two Artificial Intelligence models that have led to Persian fact-checking and stance detection.

Some of my interests include playing team sports such as soccer, volleyball, ping-pong, and Badminton, besides some individual sports like bodybuilding, camping and etc. I enjoy learning new things and sharing what I know with others. In addition, you can also see my research interests here.
